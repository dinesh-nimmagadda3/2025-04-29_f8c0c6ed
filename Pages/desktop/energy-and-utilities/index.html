Energy and Utilities

Energy and Utility

NextGen Tech is results driven, expert at managing IT infrastructure and application development for energy & utility clients and has many managed services contracts that span multiple years.


NextGen Tech provides end-to-end IT services, solutions and consulting to organizations globally to address their business needs and help them become agile, resilient and proactive. We partner with the clients for greater efficiency on all fronts, from streamlining supply chains and optimizing asset utilization to improving customer care and complying with regulations.Our solutions cater to the entire value chain for the following industries:

  • Oil, Gas and Renewable Energy
  • Utilities

NextGen Tech offers services and solutions across the utility industry, including electricity, downstream gas, water, and waste sectors. We have experience in both regulated and unregulated markets and have formed long-term client relationships

with the world’s largest utilities. We partner with utilities to deliver innovative technologies that meet current and future requirements and ensure long-term growth and success.

The challenges and pace of change facing the energy & utilities industry are unprecedented. NextGen Tech is following the world’s future energy shift whilst still coping with the current business and regulatory challenges that are present in the industry. To take full advantage as business conditions change and as operating costs are driven down so much the technologies that support your business; and this is where NextGen Tech can help.

NextGen Tech has been supplying bright ideas to its energy & utilities customers across the globe with flexible, innovative and reliable services and products. We provide solutions to tackle business issues, whether that is immediate issues such as identifying and driving new growth opportunities through customer relationship management (CRM) systems and data analytics, or ongoing issues such as the scarcity of resource through our industry solutions for smart metering and the smart city.


Our expertise in working alongside energy & utility companies allow us to offer a full range of end-to-end solutions to support today and tomorrow’s business needs.
